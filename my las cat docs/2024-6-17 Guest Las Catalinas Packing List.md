**Guest \- Las Catalinas Packing List & Tips**

**Know Before you go:**

- [ ] Make sure your Passport is not expired (Carry around a paper copy or scan)

- [ ] Download offline Google Map for the Guanacaste area (Cell can be intermittent while driving) 

- [ ] ATM/Cash (Most people do not take credit cards) You will need $100 in cash for airport transportation \+ Bring singles and $5 bills for small purchases & tips. Estimate another $100-300 for any other tours or activities you might want \- for example a beach massages is $50 \+ tip)

- [ ]  A compulsory 13% tax added to everything you buy \+ 10% service charge will always be added at restaurants  No need to add any additional tip. AMEX is not always accepted. 

- [ ] Download WhatsApp. Commonly used in CR & is helpful in making reservations, etc. (Add the **LC Concierge** **WhatsApp \# \+506 8819 9400 \-** You can reach the concierge & get quick, personalized answers to whatever you need; dinner reservations, transportation, excursion info, buggy shuttle etc**)**

- [ ] Zip up sweatshirt for the plane (The plane can get cold)

- [ ] Take photos of your luggage. Luggage is much easier to find with a photo esp w/the language barrier. If your luggage is damaged, it is much easier to make a claim with before photos

- [ ] Make a list of what you have inside of your suitcase in case your luggage is lost or to provide details if anything is missing.  

- [ ] Make a list of groceries you want to buy

- [ ] Defensive driving\! (If you are in an Accident \- don’t move the car. Call Javier WhatsApp : \+506 8837 1531\)

- [ ] Watch Electricity usage \- Electricity is Expensive. When no one is here it cost $15.00/mt when all three rooms are occupied it can be over $300/mt  **When you leave your bedroom \- turn off the room A/C & keep window blinds down**

**Immigration have ready \- Write down on a piece of paper and have handy:**

* Passport   
* **Return flight \# & Dates of trip**  
* **Hotel:** Condohotel Las Catalinas, Tempate, Santa Cruz, Guanacaste, 50304   
* Your **Profession** 

**Packing General Items**

- [ ] **\*Pack** **sunscreen and bug repellant.** You’ll need both & they are very expensive in CR

- [ ] **Water Bottle** \- one you can easily carry for hiking (or a bladder/hydration backpack) 

- [ ] **Cheap Sunglasses** **WITH a holder strap**\! (waves will carry your sunglasses away). Don’t bring your most expensive pair or bring a cheap pair for water sports

- [ ] Small little bag to hold mobile phone and sunglasses (for everyday to the beach and back)

- [ ] Motrin/Tylenol

\*We have 2 Boogie Boards, swim noodles & snorkeling equipment here
